Entrees

Aloo Paratha

This traditional, homemade flatbread is generously stuffed with a delicately spiced mixture of mashed potato and herbs, then griddled to a perfect golden brown. A popular breakfast dish across India, our Aloo Paratha offers a hearty and satisfying start to your day or a comforting addition to any meal. Paired with tangy yogurt or our house-special pickle

Cholay Bhature

Our dish features fluffy, deep-fried Bhature bread served alongside a hearty bowl of Cholay, a robust curry made from tender chickpeas simmered in a vibrant blend of spices. Finished with a squeeze of fresh lemon and a sprinkling of coriander, this soulful dish promises a burst of authentic Indian flavours

Biryani

Our royal Hyderabad chicken biryani is a luxurious journey of flavours, taking you straight to the princely state of Hyderabad in India. The tender chicken pieces, marinated in exotic spices, are slow-cooked with layers of partially cooked rice, resulting in an irresistible harmony of flavors

Saffron Rice

Indulge in the aromatic allure of our Saffron Rice. Delicate strands of saffron infuse premium basmati rice, resulting in a vibrant, flavourful dish that's as pleasing to the eye as it is to the palate.

Samosa

Dive into the world of flavour with our Vegetable Samosa. Golden, crispy pastry encases a hearty filling of spiced vegetables, offering an explosion of traditional Indian tastes. Join us in Sydney for this authentic Indian treat.

Vegetarian Curries

Aloo Beans with Potatoes

Indulge in our Aloo Beans, a simple yet delectable dish from the heart of India. This wholesome recipe marries fresh green beans with buttery potatoes, all delicately spiced and sautéed to perfection. The dish highlights the natural flavours of the vegetables, rounded off with a light touch of traditional spices

Dal Makhani

This royal delicacy features black lentils and red kidney beans slow-cooked overnight, and then simmered in a velvety gravy of butter, fresh cream, and a tantalising mix of spices. The result is a creamy, buttery dal that bursts with rich, deep flavours. Infused with aromatic spices and garnished with a dollop of cream

Egg Curry

Hard-boiled eggs gently simmered in a vibrant, spicy tomato-based sauce. The robust gravy is a medley of traditional Indian spices including turmeric, cumin, and garam masala, enveloping each egg in a blanket of rich, complex flavours. Garnished with fresh coriander

Kadhi

This distinctive dish features a smooth, golden gravy made from yogurt and chickpea flour (besan), spiced with turmeric, and tempered with a sizzling mix of mustard seeds, cumin, and dried red chillies. The result is a comforting, tangy curry, garnished with fresh coriander and served with fluffy pakoras or fritters

Mattar Paneer

This popular dish combines the soft, milky texture of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) with the sweetness of green peas, all enveloped in a lush, aromatic tomato and onion gravy. Spiced with cumin, turmeric, and a dash of garam masala, our Matar Paneer is a harmonious blend of flavours that reflects the heart of Indian home cooking

Meat Curries

Butter Chicken

Succulent pieces of chicken that have been expertly marinated in a blend of yoghurt and spices. Post grilling, the chicken pieces are simmered in a luxurious, creamy tomato-based gravy, presenting a harmonious play of smoky flavours and velvety richness

Chicken Vindaloo

Our Chicken Vindaloo features succulent chicken pieces marinated in a fiery blend of aromatic spices and tangy vinegar. The distinct heat of red chilies, combined with the robust flavors of cumin and coriander, creates an exquisite explosion of taste

Goat Curry

Our Goat Curry features tender pieces of succulent goat meat, slow-cooked to perfection in a fragrant blend of spices and aromatic herbs

Lamb Rogan Josh

Immerse yourself in the aromatic splendor of our Lamb Rogan Josh. Tender pieces of succulent lamb are cooked to perfection in a fragrant blend of spices, including cardamom, cloves, and Kashmiri red chili

Mango Chicken

Succulent chicken is cooked in a luscious mango-infused sauce, creating a harmonious balance of sweet and savory. Each bite is a tantalizing blend of tender chicken and the exotic sweetness of ripe mangoes. Pair this tropical delight with fragrant basmati rice or warm naan bread to elevate your taste buds to new heights
